<h2 align="center">Overview</h2>

Sun Life Metaverse is a modern **digital twin** of the Sun Life Waterford office, designed to improve navigation, onboarding, and hybrid collaboration.

The platform allows employees to explore the office through both **Virtual Reality** and **Web Browser** experiences, creating an engaging and practical workplace tool.

Developed as a Final Year Software Systems Development project in collaboration with **Sun Life Waterford**.
